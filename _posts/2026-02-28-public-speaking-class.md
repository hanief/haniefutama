Have you ever faced your fear?

One of my fear is speaking in public. I was a train wreck when it came to public speaking. My hands will be shaking and cold sweats coming down my forehead. I forget all my material and stumble clumsily through the presentation.

So, I decided to face my fear and be better by joining a public speaking course.

For the past month I've been going to public speaking class from [Dialogika](https://www.dialogika.co/). The class is small and focused. The instructor is very competent, experienced and approachable. The curriculum is extensive and easy to follow.

The most important thing is they provide a safe space to practice my speaking in front of audience and get a constructive feedback every time. It's so nice to have that real feedback loop.

Tomorrow is my last class and the progress has been great. I feel calmer and more confident when talking. I am still not perfect and still have much to learn. But I have been growing and it makes me happy.
