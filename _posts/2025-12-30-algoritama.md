A modern company website for a Indonesia-based software house focused on human-plus-AI engineering services. Built with Astro and Tailwind CSS, it emphasizes speed, responsive design, and a polished editorial experience through a bilingual English/Indonesian blog. The site presents core offerings in product engineering, system refactoring, and automation, supported by case-study style outcomes and technical insights.
